About me

Ryan Wood loves building software, mentoring developers, spending time in the mountains, and riding his motorcycle. He is the co-founder of MoonClerk, a company that offers a powerful, intuitive suite of tools to help businesses accept recurring payments online. He is also the co-founder of DeerLab, a company to help hunters and land managers gain more information from their trail camera photos to help in hunting and conservation.

He has a BA from Wheaton College in Philosophy and Theology and a MA from Reformed Thelogical Seminary in Counseling. Somehow, that path led him into software development and he hasn't looked back. For more info, check out his writing, projects, and photos.
